How It Works

Understanding the seamless integration of solar, AI, and blockchain technologies in our innovative platform.

Energy Generation

Solar panels generate clean energy from sunlight. SolarGridX's IoT sensors monitor production metrics and weather conditions in real-time.

AI Optimization

Our AI algorithms analyze data to optimize panel angles, predict energy output, and recommend maintenance to maximize efficiency.

Energy Storage

Excess energy is stored in advanced battery systems, with AI determining optimal charging/discharging cycles based on usage patterns and weather forecasts.

Blockchain Verification

All energy transactions are recorded on our secure blockchain, creating an immutable ledger of energy production, consumption, and trading.

Smart Contracts

Automated smart contracts execute energy trades between users, handling payments, verification, and settlement without intermediaries.

Community Benefits

The entire microgrid community benefits from increased efficiency, lower costs, and greater energy independence while reducing carbon footprint.
